Accessibility

We are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide a seamless and inclusive digital experience by following recognized accessibility standards and continuously improving the usability of our platform. Our goal is to make it easy for everyone to explore collections, access event information, and engage with our content without barriers.

We regularly review and enhance our website to support accessibility features such as clear navigation, readable design, and compatibility with assistive technologies. If you encounter any difficulty while using our website or have suggestions for improvement, we encourage you to reach out. Your feedback helps us create a more inclusive and accessible experience for all.
